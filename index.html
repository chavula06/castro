About Castro

A Leader Forged by Purpose

Castro Mwaba Mpemba is a Zambian educator, entrepreneur, and aspiring political leader known for his transformative work in education and community development. As the CEO and Founder of Growing Minds Academy (GMA) in Monze, Zambia, he has dedicated his life to nurturing young minds through quality education and leadership development.

His political journey is rooted in a simple but powerful conviction: leadership is service, not self-interest. Castro stands before the people of Fimpulu Ward not just as a candidate, but as a brother, a neighbour, and a devoted servant of the community. He does not come with empty promises — he comes with a record of action.

His vision — a self-sustaining, industrial, and inclusive community — is anchored in unity, practical action, and the deep belief that the solutions to our present and future challenges lie within our own participation and collective will.

Our Vision

The Vision for Fimpulu Ward

"Ubuyantashi Patanshi — Progress First. Development is certain when dreamers deal with the exceptional matters that unite the community."

My vision is simple yet powerful — to build a self-sustaining, industrial, and inclusive community where everyone has access to clean water, better education, reliable healthcare, job opportunities, and modern infrastructure.

1

Clean Water Access

Every household in Fimpulu Ward will have access to clean, safe water. No family should walk kilometres for a basic human right.

2

Better Education

We will expand the Growing Minds Academy model ward-wide. Quality schooling, trained teachers, and learning materials for every child — no exceptions.

3

Reliable Healthcare

A community clinic that the people of Fimpulu can rely on. Maternal health, child welfare, and accessible medicine are non-negotiable.

4

Job Opportunities

Youth entrepreneurship hubs, vocational training centres, and partnerships with Zambian businesses to create dignified employment in our own community.

5

Modern Infrastructure

Tarmacked roads, reliable electricity, and internet connectivity. Fimpulu Ward must not be left behind as Zambia modernises.

6

Inclusive Community

Every voice matters — farmers, women, youth, elders. Governance that listens, consults, and acts. An open-door leadership style, always.

Growing Minds Academy

Education Is the Foundation of Everything

GMA — Monze, Southern Province, Zambia

Founded by Castro Mwaba Mpemba, Growing Minds Academy (GMA) in Monze is more than a school — it is a launchpad for Zambia's future leaders. GMA focuses on holistic development: academic excellence, leadership skills, entrepreneurial thinking, and community responsibility.

At GMA, we believe that every Zambian child has the potential to change the world. Our model combines rigorous academics with character formation, equipping students not just to pass exams, but to lead families, businesses, and communities.

Castro's candidacy is a natural extension of GMA's mission. What he has built in Monze, he intends to scale across Fimpulu Ward — because a community that educates its children is a community that builds its own future.
